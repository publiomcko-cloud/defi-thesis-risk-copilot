# Chainlink Notes

## Oracle Risk

Oracle risk is the risk that a price feed is incorrect, delayed, manipulated, unavailable, or inappropriate for the asset being valued. DeFi lending and liquidation systems rely on price data to determine collateral value and debt safety.

## Price Feed Freshness

Price feeds should be evaluated for update frequency, deviation thresholds, supported markets, and whether the data source matches the assets and chain used by the strategy.
