## 6. LinkedIn Post Draft

```text
I am building DeFi Thesis & Risk Copilot, an AI-powered research system for DeFi strategy analysis.

The project combines RAG, LLM agents, public market data, risk scoring, and structured report generation to analyze strategies involving protocols such as Pendle, Morpho, and Aave.

The goal is not to create a trading bot. The goal is to build an explainable research copilot that helps users understand yield sources, assumptions, liquidity risk, oracle risk, liquidation risk, maturity risk, and monitoring requirements before making decisions.

Stack: FastAPI, Next.js, PostgreSQL, vector search, Hugging Face embeddings, LLM agents, Docker, and future PyTorch fine-tuning.
```
